# County Clerk Outreach — Marriage License Discount States

## The Ask
"Would you consider linking to our free guide from your marriage license page so couples know about the premarital counseling discount?"

## Why This Works
- County clerk websites have HIGH domain authority (.gov)
- Couples visit these pages right before getting married (highest intent possible)
- We're providing a genuinely useful resource (not spammy)
- Many clerk pages don't mention the discount at all — we help them serve their constituents

## Email Template

Subject: Free resource for couples applying for marriage licenses in [County]

---

Hi [Name / Office],

I run WeddingCounselors.com, a free directory that helps engaged couples find premarital counselors.

I noticed that [State] offers a marriage license fee discount for couples who complete premarital counseling (under [statute reference]). Many couples don't know about this.

We built a free guide explaining the requirements and how to find a qualifying counselor:
https://www.weddingcounselors.com/premarital-counseling/marriage-license-discount/[state]

Would you consider adding a link to this page from your marriage license information page? It could save your applicants $[amount] and help them start their marriage on the right foot.

Happy to answer any questions.

Best,
Lawrence Brennan
WeddingCounselors.com

---
