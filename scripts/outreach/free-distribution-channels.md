# $0 Distribution Channels — Ranked by Expected Impact

## Tier 1: Do This Week (highest ROI, zero cost)

### 1. County Clerk Outreach (30 emails)
- See county-clerk-outreach.md
- Expected: 3-5 link placements = .gov backlinks + direct referral traffic
- Time: 2-3 hours to personalize and send

### 2. Personal Email to 25 Claimed Counselors
- See counselor-activation-outreach.md
- Expected: 5-10 website backlinks, 2-3 colleague referrals
- Time: 1-2 hours

### 3. Reddit Posts (not spam — genuine value)
- r/weddingplanning (785k members) — "PSA: 10 states give you a discount on your marriage license if you do premarital counseling"
- r/engaged — same angle
- r/personalfinance — "TIL: Florida saves you $33 on your marriage license for premarital counseling"
- r/LifeProTips — "LPT: 10 states reduce your marriage license fee for premarital counseling"
- Link to the discount page, not the homepage
- Time: 30 minutes

## Tier 2: Do This Month

### 4. Facebook Wedding Groups
- Every major city has "Brides of [City]" or "[City] Wedding Planning" groups
- Post the discount info (not a directory plug — genuine helpful info)
- Focus on the 10 discount states first
- Time: 1 hour/week

### 5. Church Cold Email (50 churches in best cities)
- "We built a free widget for your website to help engaged couples find premarital counselors"
- Target churches that already require premarital counseling
- Link: /for-churches
- Time: 3-4 hours

### 6. Wedding Planner Outreach (local)
- Email 20 wedding planners in your best cities
- "When couples ask you about premarital counseling, here's a free directory you can point them to"
- Time: 2 hours

### 7. Google Business Profile
- Create a GBP for "WeddingCounselors.com" as an online service
- Will help with branded searches and adds legitimacy
- Time: 30 minutes

## Tier 3: Ongoing Free Channels

### 8. Quora/Answer Sites
- Answer "what is premarital counseling" questions with genuine advice + link
- Long tail traffic over time

### 9. Medium/Substack Cross-posting
- Republish blog posts to Medium with canonical tag
- Reaches Medium's existing audience (huge domain authority)
- Time: 30 min/post

### 10. Local Therapist Facebook Groups
- "Therapists of [City]" groups exist everywhere
- "Hey, I built a free directory for premarital counselors — would love to list anyone interested"
- This is the cheapest way to get more claimed counselors

## What NOT to Do
- Don't cold DM people on Instagram
- Don't post promotional content in groups that don't allow it
- Don't buy backlinks or do link exchanges
- Don't spend more time on code — the product is good enough, distribution is the bottleneck
